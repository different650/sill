Subscription FAQ

  • How do subscriptions work?

    Subscribers receive one new plant and planter each month, shipped to their delivery address. To sign up, first choose your monthly subscription theme and then choose your preferred planter color. Your subscription will cycle through an assortment of plants and planter styles.

    Note: our priority is ensuring plants arrive to you living their happiest, healthiest life. This means some plants ship fully potted in their earthenware planter while others arrive in their nursery grow pot, placed inside our earthenware planter. Simply enjoy as is or remove it from the grow pot and place it inside the planter. It's up to you!

    If you prefer to fully pot your plant, it just takes a few minutes. Our recommended approach can be found here. And if you don't have potting supplies at home, our potting supplies can be found here.

    Subscriptions are available for a duration of 3 or 6 months. They are prepaid and do not auto renew. Subscription boxes can only be delivered within the contiguous 48 states. Promotions and discounts do not apply to subscription purchases.

  • What is special about your subscriptions?

    We do the work for you-curating new easy-care and non-toxic plants for you to experience on a monthly basis and delivering them, along with a chic ceramic planter, directly to your door.

    Our plants are sourced from local growers to ensure the highest quality and to support family-owned businesses. We guarantee plants arrive in happy, healthy condition, or we replace them for free.

    Not sure how to care for your new plant? Our Care Library includes specific care instructions for each plant and our team of knowledgeable plant parents is just a message away.

  • Do subscriptions comes potted?

    No. Plants ship in their grow pot, placed inside the ceramic planter. Simply enjoy as is or remove it from the grow pot and pot it inside the planter. It's up to you!

    If you prefer to fully pot your plant, it just takes a few minutes. Our recommended approach can be found here. And if you don't have potting supplies at home, you will be able to add potting supplies to your month box with no additional shipping cost.

  • Can I gift a subscription?

    Yes-and they make great gifts! You can gift a 3-month or 6-month subscription. Simply add a gift note once the subscription is added to your cart and enter your gift recipients address as the shipping address.

  • More questions about your subscription?

    Click our Live Chat bubble at the bottom right corner or fill out our contact form and we'll get back to you as soon as possible. We recommend adding info@thesill.comto your contacts to ensure our responses land in your inbox, not your spam folder.
